DESCRIPTION

v.disaggregate.population disaggregates population data from a administrative boundary vector with population information onto a high-res raster with estimated local population. A urban area vector is used to close small gaps in the population raster in high density urban areas.

EXAMPLE

v.disaggregate.population population_vector=admin_areas population_column=POP_2020 population_raster=pop_raster_2020 urban_vector=urban_areas output=pop_raster_2020_disaggregated
